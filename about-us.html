About Us

Our Story

Founded in November 2022, Mumtaj Impex private limited, we embarked on a mission to bring premium-quality meats and fish directly to your doorstep. Sourcing directly from fishermen and trusted suppliers, our team of expert cutters ensures every product meets our high standards of freshness and cleanliness.

Our Values

At our core, we prioritize transparency, quality, and customer satisfaction. With a commitment to excellence, we strive to provide you with the finest meats and fish, backed by our money-back guarantee if you're not completely satisfied.

Our Goal

Our mission is simple: to save you time and hassle while ensuring you enjoy the best quality meats and fish. By eliminating the need for trips to the market and offering a money-back guarantee, we're dedicated to making your shopping experience effortless and satisfying.
